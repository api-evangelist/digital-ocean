DigitalOcean's Model Evaluations feature, now in Public Preview, enables teams to compare inference strategies side-by-side using the same dataset, metrics, and judge model. The tool helps organizations validate whether routing policies perform as expected in production by measuring quality, cost, and latency across different model deployment approaches before shipping to real users.
