Background: How Character.ai worked with DigitalOcean and AMD to optimize performance Character.ai , a leading AI entertainment platform with about 20 million worldwide users, wanted to optimize GPU performance and achieve lower inference costs for its application, which requires low-latency performance at large scale. They approached DigitalOcean and AMD in order to achieve this goal. Working closely together, the Character.ai , AMD, and DigitalOcean teams optimized AMD Instinct™ MI300X and MI325X GPU platforms, resulting in a 2x production inference throughput.
